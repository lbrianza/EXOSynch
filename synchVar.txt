LEPTONIDs:

Electrons:
- loose: HEEPv5.1 + pT>35
- tight: HEEPv5.1 + pT>90

Muons:
- loose: isHighPtMuon + trackIso / pt >= 0.1 + pt > 20 + |eta| < 2.4
- tight: isHighPtMuon + trackIso / pt >= 0.1 + pt > 40 + |eta| < 2.4
